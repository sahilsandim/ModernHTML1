Importance of Accessible Websites

Accessible websites are important because they allow people with disabilities to access the same information and services as everyone else. This includes news, online shopping, online banking, and government services.

Accessible websites can also improve the user experience for all users. They can also expand your audience.

Some reasons why accessible websites are important include:


Legal and ethical obligation
Expands your audience
Provides a better experience for all users
Can benefit senior citizens

Some federal and state laws require higher education institutions to make their web content accessible to people with disabilities.
